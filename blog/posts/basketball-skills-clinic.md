# Summer Basketball Skills Clinic Announced

Looking to sharpen your basketball skills during the off-season? Join us for our annual summer basketball skills clinic!

## Clinic Details

- **Dates:** July 14-18, 2025
- **Time:** 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM daily
- **Location:** Community Center Gymnasium
- **Ages:** 8-14 years old
- **Cost:** $75 per participant

## What You'll Learn

Our experienced coaches will focus on:

- Ball handling and dribbling techniques
- Shooting form and accuracy
- Defensive fundamentals
- Passing and court vision
- Game strategy and teamwork

## Registration

Registration opens May 1st and space is limited to 40 participants. Early registration is recommended!

Visit our contact page to sign up or get more information.

All participants will receive a clinic t-shirt and basketball!
