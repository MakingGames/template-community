# Congratulations to Our Winter Basketball Champions

What an incredible winter basketball season! We want to congratulate all of our players, coaches, and families for making this year so special.

## Division Champions

- **Rookies:** Team Lightning
- **Juniors:** Team Thunder
- **Intermediates:** Team Storm
- **Seniors:** Team Blaze

## Season Highlights

This season saw tremendous growth across all divisions:

- Over 200 players participated
- 45 volunteer coaches dedicated their time
- 120+ games played throughout the season

## Thank You

A special thank you to:
- All our volunteer coaches for their dedication
- Parents for getting players to practices and games
- Our referees for keeping games fair and fun
- The facilities staff for maintaining our courts

## Looking Ahead

Registration for next winter's basketball season will open in September. Watch this space for announcements!

See you next season!
