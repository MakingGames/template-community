# Fall Soccer Registration Opens August 1st

Get ready for another exciting soccer season! Registration for our fall program opens August 1st.

## Season Information

- **Season Dates:** September 6 - November 8
- **Registration Opens:** August 1
- **Registration Deadline:** August 20
- **Evaluations:** August 23 (for competitive divisions)

## Age Divisions

- Tiny Tots (Ages 4-5) - Saturday mornings
- Micro (Ages 6-7) - Saturday mornings
- Juniors (Ages 8-10) - Saturday games, weekday practices
- Seniors (Ages 11-14) - Saturday games, weekday practices

## Registration Fees

- Tiny Tots & Micro: $65
- Juniors & Seniors: $85

Fees include jersey, shin guards (first-year players), and end-of-season celebration.

## New This Year

We're excited to introduce a parent-child Tiny Tots program where parents participate alongside their children!

## How to Register

Registration will be available online starting August 1st, or visit the recreation office in person.

Questions? Contact our Soccer Coordinator through the contact page.
