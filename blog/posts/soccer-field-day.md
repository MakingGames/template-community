# Annual Soccer Field Day - Save the Date!

Mark your calendars for our annual Soccer Field Day celebration! This family-friendly event is open to all current and future soccer players.

## Event Details

- **Date:** Saturday, June 21, 2025
- **Time:** 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM
- **Location:** Memorial Park Soccer Fields
- **Cost:** FREE for all families

## Activities

- Skills challenges and obstacle courses
- Mini-games and scrimmages
- Face painting and balloon artists
- Food trucks and refreshments
- Raffle prizes and giveaways
- Meet the coaches!

## Early Registration Opportunity

Families who attend Field Day can register early for the fall season at a $10 discount!

## Volunteers Needed

We're looking for volunteers to help run activities. If you can spare a few hours, please contact the Soccer Committee.

Rain date: June 28, 2025

We hope to see you there!
