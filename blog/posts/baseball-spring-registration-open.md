# Spring Baseball Registration Now Open

We're excited to announce that registration for our spring baseball season is officially open!

## Season Details

- **Season Dates:** April 5 - June 14
- **Registration Deadline:** March 20
- **Practices:** Tuesday and Thursday evenings
- **Games:** Saturday mornings

## Age Divisions

- T-Ball (Ages 5-6)
- Minors (Ages 7-9)
- Majors (Ages 10-12)
- Seniors (Ages 13-14)

## Registration Fee

The registration fee is $85 per player, which includes:
- Team jersey and cap
- Equipment for practices
- End-of-season celebration

## How to Register

Visit our contact page to submit your registration, or stop by the recreation office during business hours.

**Early bird discount:** Register before March 10 and save $10!

We look forward to seeing everyone on the diamond this spring!
