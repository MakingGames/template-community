Baseball

Youth baseball program for all skill levels

About Our Baseball Program

Our baseball program provides a fun, supportive environment for young players to learn the fundamentals of the game while developing teamwork and sportsmanship.

Whether your child is picking up a bat for the first time or looking to improve their skills, our experienced volunteer coaches are here to help every player reach their potential.

Program Details

Ages 5-14 years old
Season Spring (April - June)
Registration Fee $XX per player
Practices Weekday evenings
Games Saturdays

Divisions

  • T-Ball (Ages 5-6): Introduction to baseball basics
  • Minors (Ages 7-9): Coach-pitch transitioning to player-pitch
  • Majors (Ages 10-12): Competitive play with full rules
  • Seniors (Ages 13-14): Advanced competitive league

What's Included

  • Team jersey
  • Team cap
  • Equipment provided for practices
  • End-of-season celebration

Volunteer Coaches Needed

Our program runs on the dedication of volunteer coaches. No experience necessary - we provide training! If you're interested in coaching, please contact us.
