Swimming

Swim lessons and summer swim team

About Our Swimming Program

Our swimming program teaches water safety and swimming skills to participants of all ages. From beginner lessons to competitive swim team, we have options for everyone.

All lessons are taught by certified lifeguards and swim instructors who create a safe, encouraging environment for swimmers to build confidence in the water.

Programs Offered

  • Parent & Tot (Ages 3-4): Water introduction with parent in pool
  • Learn to Swim Levels 1-6: Progressive skill development
  • Adult Lessons: Beginner and intermediate instruction
  • Summer Swim Team: Competitive swimming for ages 6-18
  • Private Lessons: One-on-one instruction available

What's Included

  • Certified instruction
  • Small class sizes (max 6 per instructor)
  • Progress reports for lessons
  • Swim team includes meets and end-of-season banquet

Pool Membership

Family and individual pool memberships are available for open swim throughout the summer. Contact us for membership rates and hours.
