Bowling

Youth bowling leagues and instruction

About Our Bowling Program

Our bowling program offers youth a fun, social activity that develops coordination, sportsmanship, and friendly competition. Bowlers of all skill levels are welcome!

Partnering with local bowling centers, we provide organized leagues and instruction in a family-friendly environment perfect for making new friends while learning a lifelong sport.

Program Details

Ages 6-18 years old
Season Fall & Winter
Registration Fee $XX per season
Schedule Saturday mornings
Location Local Bowling Center

Divisions

  • Bumper Bowling (Ages 6-8): Introduction with bumper rails
  • Juniors (Ages 9-12): Skill development and league play
  • Teens (Ages 13-18): Competitive league bowling

What's Included

  • Weekly league bowling (10+ weeks)
  • Shoe rental included
  • Coaching and instruction
  • Awards and trophies
  • End-of-season party

Family Fun

Parents are welcome to bowl alongside their children during open lanes! Ask about our family bowling specials.
