Track & Field

Running, jumping, and throwing events for youth

About Our Track & Field Program

Our track and field program introduces young athletes to a variety of running, jumping, and throwing events while building fitness, coordination, and healthy competition habits.

Athletes can discover their strengths across multiple events and develop speed, endurance, and technique under the guidance of experienced coaches.

Program Details

Ages 6-14 years old
Season Spring (April - June)
Registration Fee $XX per athlete
Practices Tuesday/Thursday evenings
Meets Select Saturdays

Events

  • Running: 50m, 100m, 200m, 400m, 800m, relays
  • Jumping: Long jump, high jump
  • Throwing: Shot put, softball throw
  • Special Events: Turbo javelin (ages 10+)

Age Divisions

  • Bantam (Ages 6-7): Introduction to track basics
  • Midget (Ages 8-9): Skill development across events
  • Youth (Ages 10-11): Competitive meets
  • Intermediate (Ages 12-14): Advanced competition

What's Included

  • Team uniform (singlet and shorts)
  • All equipment provided for practices
  • Entry to league meets
  • End-of-season awards ceremony
