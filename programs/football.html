Football

Youth flag and tackle football programs

About Our Football Program

Our football program teaches fundamentals, teamwork, and discipline while emphasizing player safety. We offer both flag and tackle options to accommodate different age groups and preferences.

From learning to throw a spiral to understanding team plays, our certified coaches focus on building skills, character, and a love for the game.

Program Details

Ages 5-14 years old
Season Fall (August - November)
Registration Fee $XX per player
Practices Tuesday/Thursday evenings
Games Sundays

Divisions

  • Flag Football (Ages 5-7): Non-contact introduction to football
  • Junior Flag (Ages 8-9): Advanced flag football skills
  • Tackle Minors (Ages 10-11): Introduction to tackle football
  • Tackle Majors (Ages 12-14): Competitive tackle league

What's Included

  • Complete uniform (jersey, pants, socks)
  • Equipment provided (helmets, pads for tackle)
  • USA Football certified coaching
  • End-of-season awards ceremony

Safety First

All our coaches are USA Football Heads Up certified, and we follow all recommended safety protocols. Player safety is our top priority.
