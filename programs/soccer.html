Soccer

Youth soccer program for all skill levels

About Our Soccer Program

Our soccer program emphasizes skill development, teamwork, and having fun while learning the world's most popular sport in a positive, supportive environment.

Players of all experience levels are welcome, from those just learning to kick a ball to those looking to advance their soccer skills. Our dedicated volunteer coaches focus on player development and sportsmanship.

Program Details

Ages 4-14 years old
Season Fall (September - November)
Registration Fee $XX per player
Practices Weekday evenings
Games Saturdays

Divisions

  • Tiny Tots (Ages 4-5): Introduction to soccer through play
  • Micro (Ages 6-7): Basic skills and small-sided games
  • Juniors (Ages 8-10): Skill development and team tactics
  • Seniors (Ages 11-14): Competitive league play

What's Included

  • Team jersey
  • Shin guards provided for first-year players
  • Equipment for practices
  • End-of-season celebration

Volunteer Coaches Needed

Our program runs on the dedication of volunteer coaches. No experience necessary - we provide training! If you're interested in coaching, please contact us.
