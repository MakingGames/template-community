Tennis

Lessons and leagues for youth and adults

About Our Tennis Program

Our tennis program offers instruction for all skill levels, from beginners learning to hold a racket to advanced players looking to improve their competitive game.

With access to excellent court facilities and experienced instructors, players develop proper technique, footwork, and match strategy in a fun, supportive environment.

Program Details

Ages 6+ years old (youth & adult)
Season Spring & Summer
Registration Fee $XX per session
Lessons Weekday evenings & weekends
Location Community Park Courts

Programs Offered

  • Tiny Tennis (Ages 6-8): Fun introduction using foam balls
  • Junior Development (Ages 9-12): Fundamental skills and match play
  • Teen Tennis (Ages 13-17): Advanced instruction and competition
  • Adult Lessons: Beginner to intermediate instruction
  • Adult Leagues: Singles and doubles competitive play

What's Included

  • Professional instruction
  • Court time and balls provided
  • Loaner rackets available for beginners
  • End-of-season tournament

Court Reservations

Community tennis courts are available for public use when not scheduled for lessons or leagues. Contact us for reservation information.
