Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for programs?

Registration information is announced on our News page before each season. You can also contact us directly for registration details and deadlines.

Do you offer scholarships or financial assistance?

We believe everyone should have access to our programs. Please contact us to discuss any financial concerns confidentially. We work with families to ensure cost is never a barrier to participation.

How can I volunteer?

We're always looking for volunteers! Whether you want to coach, help with events, or serve on a committee, please reach out and we'll find a role that fits your interests and availability.

What is your refund policy?

Refund policies vary by program. Generally, full refunds are available if requested before the season begins. Partial refunds may be available during the first few weeks of a program. Please contact us for specific details.

How do I report a concern or provide feedback?

We value your feedback! You can use the contact form on this page, email us directly, or speak with any board member. All concerns are taken seriously and addressed promptly.
